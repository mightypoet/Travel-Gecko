# Travel Gecko — Pricing

> Last updated: May 2026

## For Travellers

Travel Gecko is **free to use** for all travellers.

| Feature | Cost |
|---------|------|
| Sign up & create account | Free |
| Trip Wallet (create & save) | Free |
| Browse trip listings | Free |
| Book a trip | Trip price only (no platform fee) |
| Travel gear discounts | Free (unlocked via wallet balance) |

**Trip Wallet Discounts (example tiers):**
| Wallet Balance | Discount on Trips | Discount on Travel Gear |
|---------------|------------------|------------------------|
| ₹500–₹2,000 | 3% | 5% |
| ₹2,001–₹5,000 | 5% | 8% |
| ₹5,001–₹10,000 | 8% | 12% |
| ₹10,000+ | 10% | 15% |

---

## For Travel Agencies

Travel agencies pay a commission per booking made through the Travel Gecko platform.

| Plan | Details |
|------|---------|
| Listing fee | Free to list |
| Commission per booking | Contact for current rates |
| Promoted listing | Available — contact for pricing |

For agency pricing inquiries: https://travel-gecko.vercel.app/agencies

---

## Summary

- **Travellers:** Always free. Save in wallet, get discounts.
- **Agencies:** Free to list. Commission on bookings only.
